BIO

born in 1990 in the south of France. 

Multidisciplinary artist and researcher in philosophy, working and living between Lyon, Berlin and Tuscany.
Since 2017 she is following a PhD in Philosophy and Art under the direction of the Pr. Mauro Carbone (from the university of philosophy Lyon 3) and in co-direction with the research unit Contemporary Art and Historical Temporalities directed by the artist Bernhard Rüdiger (from the national superior school of fine arts of lyon, ENSBA).
Her work is closely linked to her researches, focusing on the process and questioning our relationship to technology through the use of interactive installation mixing different medias such as spatialised sounds, video, interactive technologies, sculptures and photography.
